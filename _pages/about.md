O.D. Enobabor (Omawu Diane Enobabor) is a Ph.D. Student in Geography at the Graduate Center. Diane earned her B.A. from the University of Texas- Austin in African Diaspora studies, Latin American studies, and Government. While there, she cultivated her interests in Afro-Latin American relations, focusing on Afro-Brazilian culture and identity-based development. After, she completed a master's degree in Latin American Studies at Florida International University in Miami, Florida. Her concentrations were: critical geography, Afro-Diaspora Politics and Identity, and critical development studies.
